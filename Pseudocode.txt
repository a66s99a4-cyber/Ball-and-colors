This game is a color matching jumping game.

When the game starts, the ball is at the bottom of the screen and it does not move by itself.

The ball starts at the bottom and stays still.

When the player presses the screen, the ball moves up.

When the player stops pressing, the ball falls down because of gravity.

The player must control when to press and when to release.

There are 10 horizontal moving lines above the ball.

Each line has different colors.

The lines move left and right.

Every new line is faster and has more colors.

The player presses and holds to make the ball go up.

The player releases to let the ball fall down.

The goal is to pass the ball through the correct color in each line.

The ball has one color.

Each line has many colors.

The ball must pass through the same color as the ball.

If the colors match, the ball can go to the next line.

The player loses if:

The ball falls down off the screen.

The ball touches a wrong color.

When this happens:

The ball breaks or disappears.

The game restarts from the beginning.

If the ball passes all 10 lines correctly,

A You Win message appears,

The player wins the game.

The controls are very simple (press and release).

The challenge comes from timing and colors.

The speed increases, so the game becomes harder step by step.

This game is made using simple ideas:

Press to go up

Release to fall down

Match colors to survive

Even though the rules are simple, the game is challenging and fun.
